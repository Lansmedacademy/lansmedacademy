About the Founder

Meet Lansana Coomber

Lansana Coomber is a medical student, educator and content creator on a mission to make medicine simple for students everywhere. Through LANS MED, he turns dense, intimidating textbooks into clear, visual, clinically relevant lessons — the resource he wished he had as a first-year student.

🎓 Medical Student🧑‍🏫 Medical Educator🎬 Content Creator 🚀 Founder of LANS MED💡 Simplifying Medicine📈 Student Success

Dedicated to helping students succeed academically, Lansana combines deep subject mastery with a teaching style that prioritises understanding over memorisation — building a trusted, African-founded global platform for the next generation of healthcare professionals.

The Beginning

A student frustrated by complexity

Started simplifying tough topics for classmates — realising medicine is hard not because of the content, but how it's taught.

Content Creation

LANS MED is born

Launched visual lessons across YouTube and Facebook, growing a community of learners hungry for clear explanations.
